FAQ

how does it work?

Taste the magic by selecting your favorite pizza from our menu, customize it with toppings if desired, and let us handle the rest. Sit back, relax, and enjoy your freshly baked pizza!

how long does it take for delivery?

Our delivery times typically range from 30 to 45 minutes, ensuring your pizza arrives hot and fresh right at your doorstep. Timing may vary based on location and order volume.

can I order for huge parties?

Absolutely! We cater to large parties with our special party packages. Whether you're hosting a birthday bash, corporate event, or any celebration, we've got you covered with delicious pizzas and sides to satisfy everyone's taste buds.

how much protein it contains?

Our pizzas contain varying amounts of protein depending on your choice of toppings and crust. For specific nutritional details, please refer to our menu or inquire with our staff.

is it cooked with oil?

Yes, our pizzas are prepared using high-quality oils to enhance flavor and texture, ensuring a delicious dining experience with every bite.
